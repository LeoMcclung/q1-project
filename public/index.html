Welcome to the Nahcravield

Welcome to the offical site for the Science Fiction Series, Nahcravield.

The Nahcravield, more commonly refered to as simply 'The Vield', is considered to be the single greatest technological achievement in the infinite history of the Multiverse. The Nahcravians constucted entire synthetic universes, in order to store information about literally everything that exists throughout the Multiverse. It is the most powerful asset in any situation, making it one of the most coveted things in all of existance. The series is primarily set in a Galaxy called the Asdara Galaxy, and follows the struggles of 'mortal' beings after they are suddenly faced with the sudden return of god-like 'Master' races. The Masters all yearn to have complete control of the Vield, and the violent conflicts that ensue, threaten both the Asdara Galaxy and the rest of the universe.
